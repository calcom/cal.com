Switch to this demo →
  • Headless router is prerendered for the email passed as param.email on load.
  • On email blur or select change, a new prerender is triggered.
  • The prerender queues the form response without recording it; recording happens on actual booking.
  • If the CTA is clicked before slots go stale (slotsStaleTimeMs), the prerendered modal opens instantly; otherwise slots are refetched with a skeleton shown.
  • After iframeForceReloadThresholdMs, a fresh headless router request is sent to catch routing form or ownership changes.
  • Demo values: slotsStaleTimeMs = 10s, iframeForceReloadThresholdMs = 30s (defaults: 1 min / 15 min).
  • To avoid forced reloads, re-prerender judiciously as the user interacts.
