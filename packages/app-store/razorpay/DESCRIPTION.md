Razorpay is a leading payment gateway in India that enables businesses to accept, process, and disburse payments. It supports multiple payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, wallets, and buy-now-pay-later services. With Razorpay, you can accept payments in Indian Rupees (INR) and other currencies, making it ideal for businesses operating in India and Southeast Asia.
