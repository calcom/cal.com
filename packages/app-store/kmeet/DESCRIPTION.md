Infomaniak kMeet is a secure, unlimited, and free video-conferencing service based on Jitsi technology. It offers:

- **Unlimited meetings**: No time limits on your video conferences
- **High security**: End-to-end encryption available
- **Swiss hosting**: Data hosted in Switzerland by Infomaniak
- **No account required**: Neither you nor your participants need an Infomaniak account
- **Screen sharing**: Share your screen with participants
- **Recording**: Record your meetings
- **Lobby mode**: Control who enters your meetings

## Configuration

No configuration needed! Simply install the app and start creating bookings with kMeet video links.

## How it works

When you create a booking, Cal.com automatically generates a unique kMeet room URL for your meeting. Share this link with your participants, and they can join directly from their browser without any account or installation required.

## Features

- Automatic unique meeting room generation for each booking
- No API token or configuration required
- Zero-setup installation
- Support for unlimited concurrent meetings
- Full integration with Cal.com's booking system
