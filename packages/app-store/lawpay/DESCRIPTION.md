# LawPay Payment Integration

Accept secure credit card and eCheck payments from your clients with LawPay, the payment processor designed specifically for attorneys.

## Key Features

- **IOLTA Trust Accounting Compliance**: Automatically handles trust accounting and prevents comingling of funds between operating and trust accounts
- **Secure Payments**: PCI-compliant payment processing with industry-leading security
- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Accept credit cards, debit cards, and eChecks
- **Real-time Processing**: Instant payment confirmation for your bookings
- **Attorney-Specific**: Built specifically for legal professionals with compliance in mind

## Why LawPay?

Many attorneys use LawPay as their payment processor because it:
- Automatically separates operating and trust account funds
- Complies with ABA and state bar association rules
- Provides detailed reporting for trust accounting
- Offers dedicated support for legal professionals

## Setup

1. Sign up for a LawPay account at [lawpay.com](https://lawpay.com)
2. Obtain your API credentials from the LawPay developer portal
3. Configure your operating and/or trust accounts
4. Connect LawPay to your Cal.com event types
5. Start accepting compliant payments from clients
