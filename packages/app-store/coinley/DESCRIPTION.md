Accept cryptocurrency payments directly to your wallet with Coinley. The easiest way to get paid in crypto for your Cal.com bookings.

## Why Coinley?

**Direct to Your Wallet** - Payments go straight to your wallet. No intermediaries, no waiting for payouts. You control your funds.

**Multi-Chain Support** - Accept payments on 8+ blockchains:
- Ethereum
- Binance Smart Chain (BSC)
- Polygon
- Arbitrum
- Optimism
- Avalanche
- Celo
- Base

**Multiple Cryptocurrencies** - Support for USDT, USDC, DAI, ETH, BNB, MATIC, and more.

**Lower Fees** - Only 0.5% + $0.10 per transaction. Much lower than traditional payment processors.

**Instant Settlement** - No 7-day holds. Funds are available in your wallet immediately after blockchain confirmation.

**Automatic Refunds** - Blockchain-based refund processing when bookings are cancelled.

**Real-time Tracking** - Monitor all transactions on blockchain explorers. Complete transparency.

## How It Works

1. **Connect Your Wallet** - Add your wallet addresses for supported blockchains
2. **Set Your Price** - Configure price in USD (converted to crypto automatically)
3. **Customer Books** - They pay with crypto from any wallet
4. **Get Paid Instantly** - Funds arrive in your wallet after blockchain confirmation

No custody, no complexity, just crypto payments that work.
