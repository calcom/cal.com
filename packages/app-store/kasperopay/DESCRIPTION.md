Accept Kaspa (KAS) payments for your Cal.com bookings with KasperoPay.

### Features

- **Multiple wallet support**: Kasware, Kastle, Keystone, and mobile QR payments
- **Instant payments**: Kaspa's BlockDAG confirms transactions in seconds
- **Low fees**: KasperoPay charges just 1-3.5% depending on your plan
- **No chargebacks**: Cryptocurrency payments are final
- **Direct to your wallet**: Payments go straight to your Kaspa address

### How it works

1. Install KasperoPay from the Cal.com App Store
2. Enter your KasperoPay merchant ID (get one at kaspa-store.com/merchant)
3. Enable payments on your event types
4. Customers pay with any Kaspa wallet when booking

### Get Started

Sign up for a free merchant account at [kaspa-store.com/merchant](https://kaspa-store.com/merchant) to get your merchant ID.
