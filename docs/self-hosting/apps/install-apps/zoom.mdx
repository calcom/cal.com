**Obtaining Zoom Client ID and Secret**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign into Zoom Marketplace">
    Go to [Zoom Marketplace](https://marketplace.zoom.us/) and sign in with
    your Zoom account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Build a New App">
    In the upper right, click **"Develop"** and then **"Build App"**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a General App">
    From the app type selection screen, select **"General App"** and click
    **"Create"**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name Your App">
    Provide a name for your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose User-managed App Type">
    Select **"User-managed"** as the app type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Marketplace Visibility">
    De-select the option to publish the app on the Zoom App Marketplace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create the App">
    Click **"Create"** to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Client ID and Secret">
    Scroll down to the **App Credentials** section. Copy the Client ID and
    Client Secret and add them to your `.env` file under the fields:
```
    ZOOM_CLIENT_ID
    ZOOM_CLIENT_SECRET
```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the Redirect URL for OAuth">
    Set the **OAuth Redirect URL** to:
```
    <Cal.com URL>/api/integrations/zoomvideo/callback
```

    Replace `<Cal.com URL>` with your application URL (e.g.
    `https://app.cal.com`).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Allow List and Strict Mode">
    Add the same redirect URL to the **OAuth Allow List**. Then enable
    **"Use strict mode on redirect URLs"**. Ensure it displays "saved" below
    the form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Required Scopes">
    Navigate to the **"Scopes"** tab, click **"+ Add Scopes"**, then select
    the **"Meeting"** category on the left and check the following scopes:

    - `meeting:write`
    - `meeting:read`

    Click **"Done"** to save.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Zoom Integration">
    Your Zoom integration is now ready and can be easily added in the Cal.com
    settings under **Settings → Integrations → Video**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
